# Project Submissison Instructions
Projects are due Tuesday, October 24 by midnight.

## What to Submit
Please put all your materials in a .zip file, and submit using [this google form](https://forms.gle/1LVphLHDiMMnumkE9). Include the following in your folder:

- Information about each member of your team. This should include the full name, student level (1-2 year undergraduate, upper level undergraduate, masters student, PhD student), level of experience with QIS (on a scale of 1 to 5, 5 being the most experience), and major/concentration.
- An explanation of which project you chose and why, taking into account your experience and interests. This should be no more than a paragraph long.
- An explanation of what you accomplished with your project, and any creative aspects of your project or special features/considerations that make your project unique. This can be in bullet or paragraph format, and should be 1-3 paragraphs in length.
- Project code, commented to make it legible, as .py, .ipynb, or .pdf format.
- Outputs of project code, if not included in the code itself.
- Some kind of media describing your project and what you accomplished. This can be a poster, a short video or audio clip (<2 minutes), a tutorial notebook, or something else. It should explain your project without requiring the presence of one of your team members.
